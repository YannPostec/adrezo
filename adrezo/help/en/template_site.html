Site Templates Management

With proper permissions, you can manage site templates.

Two types of site templates are defined :

  • Standardized : Enumeration of vlans and subnets. Subnets contained in a global surnet. This template has a mask positive non null
  • Non-Standardized : Enumeration of vlans. Subnets to be found in surnets list. No global surnet. Thie template has a mask equals to 0

On this page, you will find two zones :
  • An Add zone
  • the list of existing templates with their informations
Within each line, you could :
  • Delete a template when you click on . Be careful, it will delete all associated objects.
  • Modify a template when you click on
When adding entry, enter informations then click on to validate.
When modifying entry, modify informations then click on to validate.
In both cases, you could click on to cancel and go back to initial values.
A site template is defined by many fields :
  • its name
  • its mask
  • its vlans
  • its subnets
Site creation automatically creates this site vlan 0 which means 'No vlan'

You could only display lines including the string entered in Search field
