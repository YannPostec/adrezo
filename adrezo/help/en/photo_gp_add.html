Add a photo

In admin mode :

  • for room photos, click on
    Enter a mandatory name in the opening window
  • for racks, click on besides the rack name.
    Choose the position of the photo in the list.
    Only non used elements are in the list amongst (Front Up, Front Down, Back Up, Back Down).
    If you want the photo on a busy position, you must replace the photo with the appropriate action.

To add a photo for room or rack, select a new image file and click on to download your image to the server.
For browsers supporting HTML5, a progress bar appears.
Once upload finished, treatment begins (thumbnail creation and database writing.
Once treatment finished, a button appears
