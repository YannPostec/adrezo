Stock Management

With proper permissions, you can modify stock state
In the Variation column, enter a number:

  • Positive for an entry
  • Negative for an exit
Once all variations entered, click on one of

Inventory

You could also check "Inventory: " to consider variations as inventory, not real variations.
The inventory column of movements history will then display "YES"

Orders Reception

In the Ongoing column, if the type has elements in ongoing order, you'll find an icon
By clicking on it, you add in the stock the number of items ordered
The inventory column of movements history will then display "Reception"
