Subnets Management

With proper permissions, you can manage subnets.

On this page, you will find two zones :
  • An Add/Modify zone
  • the list of existing subnets with their informations
Within each line, you could :
  • Delete a subnet when you click on . Be careful, it will delete all associated objects.
  • Modify a subnet when you click on
When adding or modifying, enter or modify informations then click on to validate.
You could also click on to cancel, going back to empty values.
A subnet is defined by many fields :
  • its site
  • its starting IP
  • its subnet mask
  • its name
  • the gateway IP address (as information to the user)
  • the broadcast IP address (as end limit to available IP search)
  • its vlan ID if any. If none, choose ID 0 which means 'No vlan'
The broadcast IP address is calculated automatically when subnet ip and mask are entered.
In case of add or modification, the ip/mask you entered are checked for subnet address validity.
