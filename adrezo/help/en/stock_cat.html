Furnitures Categories Management

With proper permissions, you can manage categories.

On this page, you will find a table with :
  • An Add zone on the first line
  • the list of existing categories with their informations on the next lines
Within each line, you could :
  • Delete a category when you click on . Be careful, it will delete all associated objects.
  • Modify a category when you click on
When adding or modifying, icons appears.
Enter or modify informations then click on to validate.
You could also click on to cancel, going back to initial values.
A category is defined by only one field :
  • its name
