Racks Management

With proper permissions, you can manage racks of a given set.

On this page, you will find two zones :
  • An Add zone
  • the list of existing racks with their informations
Within each line, you could :
  • Delete a rack when you click on . Be careful, it will delete all associated objects.
  • Modify a rack when you click on
When adding entry, enter informations then click on to validate.
When modifying entry, modify informations then click on to validate.
In both cases, you could click on to cancel and go back to initial values.
A rack is defined by many fields :
  • its site
  • its room
  • its set
  • its name
  • position of this rack in the set

You could only display lines including the string entered in Search field
