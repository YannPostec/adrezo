Network Availability Statistics

This page display statistics about availability rate of network devices.
This data comes from Cacti
Data are ordered by client, then by entity (site, group, ...) and then by devices if needed.

You can select a month and year or choose the All item as month to display the entier year.

You can choose to compare this result with another period of time or disable the functionnality with Activate comparison

When your selection is made, click on to display the statistics.

By clicking on , a full excel export is runned.

N.B. : Page loads automatically with last available month data in comparison with previous month.
