Sites Management

With proper permissions, you can manage sites.

On this page, you will find two zones :
  • An Add/Modify zone
  • the list of existing sites with their informations
Within each line, you could :
  • Delete a site when you click on . Be careful, it will delete all associated objects.
  • Modify a site when you click on
When adding or modifying, enter or modify informations then click on to validate.
You could also click on to cancel, going back to empty values.
A site is defined by many fields :
  • its context
  • its code
  • its name
Site creation automatically creates this site vlan 0 which means 'No vlan'
