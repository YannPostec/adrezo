Vlans Management

With proper permissions, you can manage vlans.

On this page, you will find two zones :
  • An Add/Modify zone
  • the list of existing vlans with their informations
Within each line, you could :
  • Delete a vlan when you click on
  • Modify a vlan when you click on
When adding or modifying, enter or modify informations then click on to validate.
You could also click on to cancel, going back to empty values.
A vlan is defined by many fields :
  • its site
  • its id
  • its name
Site creation automatically create a vlan 0 which means 'No Vlan'
