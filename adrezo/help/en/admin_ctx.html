Context Management

With proper permissions, you can manage contexts

On this page, you will find a table with :
  • An Add zone on the first line
  • the list of existing contexts with their informations on the next lines
Within each line, you could :
  • Delete a context when you click on . Be careful, it will delete all associated objects.
  • Modify a context when you click on
When adding or modifying, icons appears.
Enter or modify informations then click on to validate.
You could also click on to cancel, going back to initial values.
A context is defined by only one field :
  • its name

N.B :

  • Last context in list cannot be deleted.
  • When a new context is created, you must logout and log again to get proper rights on this new context.
