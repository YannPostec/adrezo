Photo Album

To display photos of a given room, start by selecting a site in the first scroll list
it allow you to choose the romm on a second list
Many zones appears that you could display or not :

  • Room Photos
  • One or many racks sets
In racks set, the rack name have sometimes the text "(Recto)" indicating that back photos are available.
To see them, click on the "(Recto)" which turns into "(Verso)"

In any case, click on a photo to display it in a new browser window.

Slideshow Photos

In each zone, you could click on to display a slideshow with photos of the room or rack sets.

This slideshow start automatically, time between each photo is configured in user preferences
Photo name as upload date are displayed overprinted.
You can use controles to pause/resume slideshow or to go to next or previous photos.
Keyboard left and right arrows could be used to navigate in photos as well.
