Subnets Management in site template

With proper permissions, you can manage subnets in site template.

On this page, you will find two zones :
  • An Add zone
  • the list of existing subnets with their informations
Within each line, you could :
  • Delete a subnet when you click on
  • Modify a subnet when you click on
When adding entry, enter informations then click on to validate.
When modifying entry, modify informations then click on to validate.
In both cases, you could click on to cancel and go back to initial values.

You could only display lines including the string entered in Search field

A subnet is defined by many fields :
  • its name
  • its subnet ip
  • its mask
  • its optionnal gateway ip
  • its vlan
  • its broadcast ip, automtically computed
  • its surnet list for a non-standardized site
N.B : #CODE# will be replaced with site code, #SITE# with site name

Subnets in standardized site template

Enter a mask for all possible subnet IP to be computed.
They are all subnets possible within the site surnet, computed on a ip base of 0.0.0.0
Warning existing subnets ip are not cleared from the computed list

Subnets in non-standardized site template

The subnet will be the first available in the given surnet list, its value is always 0.0.0.0
